Donations & Support


Donations from private sponsors have are a key part for furthering our research into genetics and genomics of human cancer. Philanthropic support is one of the most powerful means that our group can have to develop studies into new research areas. If you are interested in giving a gift to support research in the Carvajal-Carmona laboratory, please contact us at (530)-752-9654 or lgcarvajal@ucdavis.edu, or visit the UC Davis Health System giving page.
