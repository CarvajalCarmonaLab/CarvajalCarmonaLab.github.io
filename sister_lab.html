Our Colombian Collaborators


At the Carvajal-Carmona lab, we are always working alongside other labs in order to obtain the best understanding of the types of cancer that are studied. Our sister lab is located in Colombia, at the University of Tolima, Ibague. They play a huge role in the research we do at our lab. They also have quite a bit going on themselves.

Two of our graduate researchers, Ana and Angel, travel between the two labs. They do research in both labs as well as assisting in maintaining a solid connection between the two collaborators. Ana is involved in a cytogenetics group, which can be seen in some of the pictures below.
